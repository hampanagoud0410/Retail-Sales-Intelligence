# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Title
Retail Sales Intelligence Platform

## Business Problem
RetailMart generates thousands of sales transactions every day across multiple stores. Business leaders currently rely on static spreadsheets, making it difficult to monitor sales performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

## Objectives

- Monitor sales performance
- Track revenue and profit
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare regional sales
- Monitor product returns
- Forecast future sales trends

## Stakeholders

- CEO
- Sales Manager
- Finance Team
- Marketing Team
- Regional Managers

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Profit Margin
- Monthly Sales Growth
- Return Rate

## Expected Outcome

Develop a web-based analytics platform that provides interactive dashboards, business insights, and supports strategic decision-making.
